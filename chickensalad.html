Caesar Salad

A fresh and crunchy salad tossed with creamy dressing, crisp lettuce, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 1 romaine lettuce
  • ½ cup croutons
  • 2 tbsp Caesar dressing
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan
  • 1 boiled egg (optional)

Procedure:

  • Wash and chop lettuce.
  • Toss with dressing and croutons.
  • Top with Parmesan and egg slices.
  • Serve chilled.

🔥 Calories: ~180 kcal per serving
